CSV to Datalake Formatter

A high-performance CSV to SQL datalake formatter for making UNF databases out of large datasets for big data.

Ever wondered why you people use Python instead of SQL for data analysis? Part of that is the community around it. The second part is the lack of tooling.

The CSV to Datalake Formatter is a prototype for a suite of SQL tools meant to match the performance of Pandas. It's designed to take in a large CSV file at a time and load it into a datalake in chunks; effectively reducing memory requirements and maximizing speed through multi-threading.

And the best part? It's designed to work on your work computer. Or server. Free of charge. Just plug in the CSV file and it'll work on your own development system even without the internet.
