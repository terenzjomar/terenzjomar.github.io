About Terenz

There's more to computation than 1's and 0's

His works with sensors and microcontrollers bring in a lot of usable data which he uses to create exciting graphs and informative data analyses.

Part of his strengths come from his knowledge of optimizing for memory constraints in edge-computing and big data applications.
